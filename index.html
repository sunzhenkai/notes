Wii's Talking Ground

欢迎来到 Wii 的世界。

Welcome to Wii's world.

这是一个由文字构建的世界，纵如潺潺涓流，也能感受到其间微小的能量、情绪的波动、 缓慢的成长。既有内心的独白，也有技术的积淀，希望这些文字能有些许用处。

This is a world built with words — like a gentle stream, you can still feel the subtle energy, emotional ripples, and slow growth within. A mix of inner monologues and technical accumulations; I hope these words prove somewhat useful.

大模型正在重构整个文字世界，这既让文字变得廉价，也使得别具一格的文字显得弥足珍贵。

Large language models are reshaping the entire world of text, making words cheap yet rendering truly distinctive writing all the more precious.

对于软件开发从业者，大模型已成为绕不开的课题，从 2025 年开始积极拥抱这一新质生产力。这同时也带来了生成文字、手写文字难以区分的问题， 为此我将使用两个资源做硬隔离。

For software developers, LLMs have become an unavoidable topic. Since 2025, I've been actively embracing this new productive force. This also brings the challenge of distinguishing AI-generated text from handwritten text — to address this, I maintain two strictly separated resources.

note.wii.pub 是我从学生时代便开始积累的文档站点，内容涵盖编程技术、生活技巧与个人喜好。 后续我也会借助 LLM 来更好地总结知识，这部分内容同步发布于此。

note.wii.pub is a documentation site I've been building since my student days, covering programming techniques, life hacks, and personal interests. I continue leveraging LLMs to better synthesize knowledge, with content synced and published here.

与 Notes 做了硬隔离的另一个资源是 blog.wii.pub， 基于 Halo 搭建，手写文字为主体，大模型生成文本则通过引用标注。

Another resource, strictly isolated from Notes, is blog.wii.pub — built on Halo, with handwritten text as the main body and LLM-generated content attributed via citations.
